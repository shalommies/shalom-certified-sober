Introducing Sober

We're testing Sober, a brand new way to get Roblox on your Linux desktop. It's a novel approach, separate to other compatibility software like Wine, Android emulators, or virtual machines. Sober works by creating a specialized runtime just for Roblox's code, ensuring compatibility.

We'd like to stress that Sober is an experimental project. It's a passion project rather than production-grade code. Sober's runtime incurs a performance penalty compared to Windows. While the results vary for everyone, generally Sober offers 1/3rd the performance of Windows.

Sober is unfortunately closed-source. This could change in the future.

We distribute Sober as a Flatpak package. To use it, just make sure Flatpak is installed and install the package. Then, open your favorite game. You'll be prompted to install Roblox. After that, you may visit any experiences on the platform, interact with other users, etc.

Sober is a labor of love, and we'd love for you to try it out! We hope you enjoy your time.
